Instructions

The purpose of this puzzle is to order the tiles, which are initially scrambled, from the smallest to the largest number as shown in figure "Goal" below. You can move a tile onto the empty slot by clicking on the tile. A tile can move only if it is next to the empty slot.

The Task

Each move on the board has a fixed cost of 1. Your task is to arrange the tiles in ascending order, with the empty slot being on the bottom right position of the puzzle, with minimal cost.

Note

At any stage, you can undo as many steps as you wish by repeatedly clicking the "UNDO“ button.

Your payment:

You get 50 cents fixed payment and a BONUS that will depend on how close your estimate is to the true minimal cost to solve the puzzle.
