Instructions

The purpose of the game is to reorder the numbers by ascending order.

The order in the puzzle is mixed up, your purpose is to get the ascending order back (from 1,2,...). You can only move a numbered tile to the empty slot by clicking on this number. A number can move only if he is next to an empty slot.

Possible Actions

Movement: You can move a number to an empty slot by clicking on the number ; only numbers that are next to an empty slot can move. You cannot move a number that are not next to an empty slot.

The Task

Each move on the board has a fixed cost of 1. Your task is arrange the numbers in ascending order, empty slot is last, with minimal cost.

Note

At any stage, you can backtrack as many steps as you wish by clicking the "UNDO“ button.

Your payment:

Your get 50 cents fixed payment and a BONUS that will depend on how close your estimate is to the true minimal cost to complete the goal pattern.
